About SkyCast

SkyCast is a modern, responsive weather application designed to give you precise weather data instantly. Whether you're planning your day or a future trip, our dashboard provides you with all the essential atmospheric information you need.

Powered by WeatherApi's API, features include real-time temperature tracking, detailed weekly forecasts, and seamless responsive design that adapts beautifully to any device.
